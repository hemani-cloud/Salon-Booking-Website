Our Story

Luxe Salon was founded in 2010 by Isabella Rossi with a vision to create a sanctuary where clients could experience the ultimate in beauty and relaxation. Starting as a small boutique salon, we've grown into a premier destination for hair, skin, and nail care, known for our exceptional service and talented team.
